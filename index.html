I wasn't the only one concerned
about the unreliability of the
macros in both MyFitnessPal and
Carb Manager.

I started Keto in July. MyFitnessPal seemed pretty cool
. Spent hours setting it up and entering recipes only to
discover the macros were not reliable due to all the
contributions by the community. I was so frustrated. Then I
found a review of several apps (on the Keto Mojo website)
and discovered I wasn't the only one concerned about
unreliability of the macros on both MFP and CarbManager
(tried that too). Behold Cronometer! I love this app. It draws
from nutrition databases with minimal community
contributions so much, much more reliability. There are
many more free features. The user interface is easy to
navigate. I'm very happy with Cronometer.

plate. I bought gold within minutes. This is set up great,
easy to use, links with my FitBit. I could go on and on.
